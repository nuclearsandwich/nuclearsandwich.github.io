What I tell other people about me

On any given weekday Steven! is a GitHub Supportocat and a student of computer science, mathematics, and latte art. When Friday rolls around he's passionate Ruby hacker who loves contributing to open source projects. He teaches and contributes to KidsRuby, CoderDojo, and RailsGirls and is also on the core team for RailsBridge. He has spent more than a few evenings scrabbling to get out of the Bronze League in StarCraft II. Most importantly, Steven! is a dedicated engineer with a passion for creating beautiful things.

What I think about me

I don't know what I'm doing, but I feel like I do when I teach other people what little I know. So I do that as much as I possibly can.
