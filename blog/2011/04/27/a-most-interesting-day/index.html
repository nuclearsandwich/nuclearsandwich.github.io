A Most Interesting Day

Apr 27th, 2011

Today was an interesting day. I helped to start an International holiday in Corenominal Day, stopped for coffee when I was already running late despite the fact that I loathe being tardy, tolerated and even enjoyed some aspects of classes today, and got stung by a wasp that got trapped in my beard while I was cycling home. All in all, today was quite eventful. Though I still didn’t get as much done as I {needed,intended,wanted}. Oh well, there’s always tomorrow.
